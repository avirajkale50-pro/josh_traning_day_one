Describe a real-world system where:
Soft deletes are required.
Hard deletes are preferred.
Answer : 
Soft Deletes Required – Healthcare Management System
    a.Patient records cannot be permanently removed due to legal and regulatory requirements system
    b.Medical history must be preserved for future treatments 
    c.Records may need to be restored if deleted by mistake
    d.Data is usually marked as `deleted_at` instead of deleting permanently

Hard Deletes Preferred – OTP / Online Meeting Chats
    a.OTP and meetings are temporary and time-bound
    b.Old data of OTP are not need and also the meeting notes is there so the chats are not necessary 
    c.Reduces security risks by not retaining sensitive data as chats can contain sensitive information about company
